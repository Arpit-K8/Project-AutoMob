Tire Warning Signs to Watch For

Worn Tread

Insufficient tread depth reduces traction and increases stopping distance

Uneven Wear

Indicates alignment issues or suspension problems requiring attention

Low Pressure

Underinflated tires reduce fuel efficiency and increase wear

Vibration

Steering wheel vibration suggests balancing or alignment issues

Tire Maintenance & Safety Tips

Monthly Checks

  • Check tire pressure when cold
  • Inspect tread depth with penny test
  • Look for cracks, bulges, or damage
  • Check for uneven wear patterns

Driving Tips

  • Avoid sudden starts and stops
  • Maintain proper following distance
  • Avoid potholes and road debris
  • Rotate tires every 6,000-8,000 miles
