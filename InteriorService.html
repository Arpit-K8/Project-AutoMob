AC System Maintenance Guide

Signs Your AC Needs Service

  • Weak or warm air flow
  • Unusual odors from vents
  • Strange noises when AC runs
  • Excessive moisture or fogging
  • Water leaking inside the cabin
  • AC takes too long to cool
  • Frequent cycling of the AC compressor
