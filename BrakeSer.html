Brake System Warning Signs

Squealing Noise

High-pitched squealing when braking indicates worn brake pads needing replacement

Vibration

Pulsating brake pedal or steering wheel vibration suggests warped rotors

Soft Pedal

Spongy brake pedal feel may indicate air in brake lines or low fluid levels

Warning Light

Illuminated brake warning light requires immediate professional attention
