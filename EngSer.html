Engine Warning Signs to Watch For

Check Engine Light

Dashboard warning light indicates engine system issues requiring immediate attention

Unusual Noises

Knocking, rattling, or grinding sounds from the engine compartment

Poor Performance

Reduced power, hesitation during acceleration, or rough idling

Fluid Leaks

Oil, coolant, or other fluid puddles under your parked vehicle
